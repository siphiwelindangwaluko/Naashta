Our Restaurant

Naashta isn't a cut-out character. Our samoosas aren't like that. Moreover, it is undeniably not the way we live. We don't make do with anything less than delicious food around here. In addition, we don't just check-in. Not when we can also become our best selves, make friends, and have a good time while we're at it. We're a fast-food chain that doesn't take up much space. We make people happy by filling their stomachs.

We don't pre-bundle your samoosas at Naashta; instead, we wait until you tell us what you need, and then we get to work. It does take a little longer than a grab-and-go samoosa, but that is what makes us unique.
